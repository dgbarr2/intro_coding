How to use these notes

These notes include just the main points that we will cover during the lecture sessions. They are not detailed enough to serve as a standalone tutorial, largely because they do not explain what you should do if something goes wrong or does not work - these things we will cover during the sessions.

Setting up the coding window.

  1. Click the 3 vertical dots at the top right corner.
  2. Click 'More tools'.
  3. Click 'Developer tools'.
  4. On the menu bar at the top of the new window that has (or should have) opened on the right, click 'Console'.
  5. Drag the left hand border of the window to the left to widen it.
